“With a global urge on sustainbale development, it is necessary to create a framework for sustainable development. With the core ideals of Less Waste, Safer Environment and Renewable Development, John Warner and Paul Anastas (Anastas & Warner, 1998) first developed a set of rules to standardise sustainable research and practise. This article serves an introductory piece to this framwork, summarising expert knowledge and insight onto why such rules are most important for the future of the chemistry industry.”
